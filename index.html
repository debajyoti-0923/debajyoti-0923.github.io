instructions :

1. Starting the Game:
   -> The large game board starts empty.
   -> Players decide who goes first, typically X.
   -> The first player places their marker (X) in any of the 9 small boards' cells.
   -> After that, the game proceeds according to the following rules:

2. Game Rules:
   -> The cell where a player makes a move in a small board determines where the opponent must make their next move on the large board.
   -> For example, if X places their marker in the top-right cell of a small board, O must make their next move in the top-right small board of the large board.

3. Winning a Small Board:
   -> To win a small Tic-Tac-Toe board, a player must get three of their markers in a row, column, or diagonal, just like in classic Tic-Tac-Toe.
   -> When a player wins a small board, they claim it by marking it with their X or O.
   -> If the small board is already won or full, the player can choose any other open small board.

4. Winning the Game:
   -> To win the entire game, a player must win three small Tic-Tac-Toe boards in a row, column, or diagonal on the large board.
   -> If all small boards are filled, and no player has won, the game is a draw.

4. Game Continues:
   -> Players continue taking turns, playing in the small boards as determined by their opponent's previous move.
   -> If a player is sent to a small board that has already been won or is full, they can choose any other open small board.
